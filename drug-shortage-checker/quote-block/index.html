“I heard about drug shortages, but didn't know that chemo treatments were falling into that category too. I guess you're pretty naive going into it. It's so much information thrown your way. I guess I just tried to stay positive. Roll with the punches.”

- Darcey Filus

Breast cancer patient

“Because of my rare disease, any change can cause me to go into anaphylactic shock. When the shortage happened, my fear was, ok, my blood sugars are going to go nuts, I'm going to end up in anaphylaxis again, and I'm going to end up in the hospital on a vent.”

- Tammie Largent-Phillips

Systemic mastocytosis patient

“It does make you realize that any type of situation like this is just unacceptable. It shouldn't be happening in a society that prides itself on being the richest and most prosperous society, that things like this can fall through the cracks.”

- Dylan Rice

ADHD patient
